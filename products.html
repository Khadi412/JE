Roasted Vermicelli

Authentic Pakistani Sevaiyan

Pakistan's vermicelli, known locally as 'Sevaiyan,' is a popular type of pasta, particularly in desserts. It's a thin, round noodle made from wheat flour, water, and salt — a key ingredient in traditional Pakistani sweets like Sheer Khurma.

Expertly roasted to perfection, each strand embodies a symphony of flavours and textures. Whether preparing Sheer Khurma or savory Pulao, our Roasted Vermicelli transforms ordinary meals into extraordinary feasts.
